# Format Options

Choose the format that best fits the user's idea, goal, and audience. Recommend one option in a single sentence, then let the user confirm or choose another.

## Step-by-step playbook

A clear numbered process that teaches the viewer how to do something specific.

Best for tutorials, frameworks, how-to content, lead magnets, and educational products.

Structure: hook image with the promise, one step per image, final recap plus CTA.

## Mini tutorial or how-to

A short visual tutorial showing a tool, feature, method, or workflow in action.

Best for software tutorials, AI workflows, productivity tips, and content creation processes.

Structure: hook image with the outcome, one action per image, final result plus CTA.

## Myths vs. facts or common mistakes

Challenges a common belief or shows what people do wrong and what actually works.

Best for education, persuasion, positioning, objections, and belief-shifting content.

Structure: hook image with the tension, one myth or mistake per image, correction, CTA.

## Checklist, cheat sheet, or framework

Save-worthy reference content people can return to.

Best for frameworks, question lists, templates, repeatable systems, and tactical references.

Structure: hook image with the promise, one item per image, final complete list plus CTA.

## Before and after transformation

Shows a transformation such as a redesign, rewrite, result, audit, or improvement.

Best for case examples, visual proof, client work, and service-based offers.

Structure: result, before, after, breakdown, CTA.

## Day or week in my life

A personal timeline of a routine or behind-the-scenes experience.

Best for creators, founders, freelancers, and personal brands.

Structure: intro image, one moment per image, final takeaway plus CTA.

## Behind the scenes or process

Shows how something was created.

Best for launches, campaigns, product creation, client projects, and creative work.

Structure: end result, idea, draft, feedback, final, CTA.

## Tools, stack, or resources

Shares useful tools, resources, templates, or recommendations.

Best for creator stacks, AI tools, productivity resources, and business systems.

Structure: hook image, one tool per image, summary plus CTA.

## Case study or story

A narrative arc showing a real result or lesson.

Best for authority, credibility, client work, and product or service proof.

Structure: result, problem, approach, outcome, lesson, CTA.

## List or ranking

One item per image with a bold title and a short takeaway.

Best for tips, lessons, recommendations, rankings, mistakes, and ideas.

Structure: hook image, one item per image, final list plus CTA.
